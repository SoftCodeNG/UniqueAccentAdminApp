Lesson 1
SPEAKING IS DIFFERENT FROM COMMUNICATION, You can Speak without communicating and this is what many people do not understand. A lot of People think that speaking with good diction is about faking an accent or rolling your tongue, NO! It is never about rolling your tongue or faking an accent, It is about accurate and effective communication such that anyone from any part of the world can understand you clearly. This is what a lot of people do not know. As a Public speaker, A radio/TV presenter, A voice over artiste, Customer service representative
